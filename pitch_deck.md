# Slide 1: The Problem
### Inefficient and Expensive Last-Mile Delivery
- **High Delivery Costs**: Standard quick commerce apps charge high delivery and platform fees per order, making daily essentials expensive.
- **Logistics Inefficiency**: Delivery riders make multiple trips to the exact same apartment complexes throughout the day, wasting time and fuel.
- **Inventory Mismatch**: Local vendors struggle to predict demand for large communities, leading to stockouts of daily necessities (like milk and bread) during peak hours.
<!-- slide -->
# Slide 2: Our Solution & Key Features
### Hyper-Local Delivery through Density Optimization
We are building a hyper-local delivery platform that clusters orders by specific apartment blocks and time slots (Morning, Afternoon, Evening) to drastically reduce delivery costs.

**Key Features:**
- **Community-Centric Ordering**: Customers order using their specific City, Apartment, and Flat number.
- **Time-Slot Clustering**: Deliveries are batched together, dropping the cost per order from ₹40 to ~₹1.20.
- **Vendor Intakes**: Local vendors can instantly scan barcodes to add inventory into the Master Catalog.
- **AI Forecasting**: Predictive insights track high demand and low stock to alert vendors ahead of time.

# Slide 4: Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
### Who are we building for?
- **Primary Audience**: Residents of large gated communities and high-density apartment complexes.
- **Demographics**: Middle to high-income families and working professionals who order daily essentials (groceries, dairy, snacks).
- **Secondary Audience (Vendors)**: Local shop owners and distributors near these complexes looking to digitize their inventory and bulk-sell without high logistics overhead.
